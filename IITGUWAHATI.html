MODE OF ADMISSION

  1. It is a two-step process. One needs to qualify the JEE (Main) exam and then take the JEE (Advanced) exam.
  2. One must have secured at least 75% marks (65% for SC/ST/PwD) in Class XII OR One must be within the category-wise top 20 percentile in his/her respective Class XII board examination. Read JEE Advanced Performance Criteria in Class XII.
  3. Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Advanced) through JoSAA Counselling.
