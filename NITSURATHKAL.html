Mess Charge (per Semester) Rs: 20,000
Total Rs: 30,500

FEE WAIVERS

  1. 100% Tuition Fee waiver for SC/ST/PH students.
  2. Full remission of the Tuition Fee for General and OBC students whose family income is less than Rs.1 lakh per annum.
  3. Remission of 2/3rd of the Tuition Fee for General and OBC students whose family income is between Rs.1 lakh to Rs.5 lakh per annum.
