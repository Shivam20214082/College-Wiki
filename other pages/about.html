About Us

collegeWiki went live in June 2013 with a goal of helping the engineering aspirants. We started by providing detailed information about different Engineering Colleges & entrance Exams. Soon, we added news section for providing the latest news about each exam & their counselling schedule. And there are some questions that need discussion, so we created a Forum where students can easily ask, answer & discuss questions.
