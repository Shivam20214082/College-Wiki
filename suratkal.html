    MODE OF ADMISSION

    1. One must have secured at least 75% marks (65% for SC/ST/PwD) in Class XII OR One must be within the category-wise top 20 percentile in his/her respective Class XII board examination. Read JEE Advanced Performance Criteria in Class XII.
    2. Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Mains) through JoSAA Counselling.

    FEE WAIVERS

    1. 100% Tuition Fee waiver for SC/ST/PH students.
    2. Full remission of the Tuition Fee for General and OBC students whose family income is less than Rs.1 lakh per annum.
    3. Remission of 2/3rd of the Tuition Fee for General and OBC students whose family income is between Rs.1 lakh to Rs.5 lakh per annum.
